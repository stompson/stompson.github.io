About Me

I received my PhD in Social Psychology from the University of Michigan in 2016. Since then, I have been working as a postdoctoral scholar at the University of Pennsylvania and U.S. Army Research Laboratory. I am currently the Robert J. Eichelberger Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellow in the Human Research and Engineering Directorate at the U.S. Army Research Laboratory and a Visiting Scholar in the Bioengineering Department at the University of Pennsylvania.
