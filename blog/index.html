Data and Human Behavior

How can data help us better understand human behavior? What insights can we derive about why people behave in certain ways, and what can we learn that will improve decision-making in the future? In this blog, I will use Python and R to scrape web data about human behavior in a variety of domains (sports, politics, music, movies) and use stats to identify important behavioral patterns and make recommendations for how this behavior could be "better". This blog is a side-project and I hope to post weekly, but articles might be more sporadic at times.
